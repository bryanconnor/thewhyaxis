## OpenVis Conference - 2014

OpenVis Conference, organized and hosted by (link:http://www.bocoup.com/ text:Boucoup), is in its second year. The conference focuses on creating visualizations for the web and openly sharing technologies and techniques.

## Tapestry Conference - 2014

(link:http://www.tapestryconference.com/ text:Tapestry Conference) also had its second event this year. Organized by (link:http://www.tableausoftware.com/ text:Tableau), this conference has a focus on storytelling and features a great mix of talks from speakers inside and outside of the visualization world.
